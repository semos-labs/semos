# Attyx v0.2.25

## Improvements

- **Session picker polish** — the active session now always appears at the top of the list, and the cursor starts there too. The macOS shortcut has moved to `Cmd+Shift+S`.

## Fixes

- **Reliable session restore** — your last session is now saved more consistently when switching tabs, changing pane focus, or shutting down. Reopening Attyx will correctly restore the session you were last using.
