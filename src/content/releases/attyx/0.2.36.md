# Attyx v0.2.36

## Smoother Upgrades, Smarter Launches

**More reliable session migration** — Fixed a race condition during hot-upgrade where a competing daemon could steal the socket, causing the legitimate one to exit and take your sessions with it. The upgrade handoff now has better coordination, retries, and safeguards to keep your sessions alive through updates.

**Better starting directory** — When you launch Attyx from Finder, Spotlight, or the Dock, it now opens in your home directory instead of `/`. Previously, macOS would hand the terminal a working directory of `/`, which isn't where anyone wants to start.
